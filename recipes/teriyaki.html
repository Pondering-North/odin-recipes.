Crockpot Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

"Boneless skinless chicken breasts and homemade teriyaki sauce served over rice make this crockpot teriyaki chicken a perfect weeknight meal. Itâ€™s so easy and delicious!"

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup honey
    • 1/4 cup brown sugar 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup dry red wine
  • Rice or quinoa, for serving
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish

Directions

  1. Lightly spray the bottom and sides of slow cooker with nonstick spray, if desired. Place chicken breasts on bottom of slow cooker.
  2. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, honey, brown sugar, and garlic in a small bowl or measuring cup. Pour over chicken breasts.
  3. Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours or 6 to 8 hours on Low setting, until chicken breasts are tender and completely cooked.
  4. Remove chicken from slow cooker and shred with forks. Set aside.
  5. Pour sauce into small saucepan. Combine cornstarch with water, and add to sauce. Over medium heat, bring sauce to a low boil, stirring often, and cook another minute, until sauce is thickened. Pour thickened sauce over shredded chicken.
  6. Serve over rice or quinoa, topped with sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Enjoy!
